WHAT IS THE AFFILIATE PROGRAM?

The Shoppingbee affiliate program is a great way for you to earn commissions by placing product banners or links on your website to refer users to the Shoppingbee.com website. You can earn up to 20% every time a user clicks on the banner/link and makes a purchase on our site.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How much can I earn via the Shoppingbee affiliate program?
Shoppingbee.com pays commission to affiliates depending on category of product sold via their website, The referral rates are stated to the right.
How does it work?
Refer visitors from your website to Shoppingbee.com using a host of professionally designed tools. You earn money whenever visitors follow links from your website, to Shoppingbee.com, and make purchase(s). With the range of options that the Affiliate program arms you with, you will find multiple avenues to link up your website with www.Shoppingbee.com. Add rich customised content to spruce up and add value to your website. The low-risk, high-reward environment for both parties makes for an offer that you cannot let go of.
What are the different banners/links I can use?
a. Banners can be created for individual products across all categories. Easy to generate, and customizable over a wide range of colours and dimensions, our banners are the simplest way to link to Shoppingbee, and add compelling content to your website.

- Search for any individual product across any category
- Customize according to your requirements
- Highlight the code in the text box, and copy
- Paste the code in your website

b. Links can be created for any product listed on Shoppingbee.com.. Similar to banners, links too can be generated and customised according to your needs.

- Search for any product across any category
- Change link text if required
- Highlight the code in the text box, and copy
- Paste the code in your website

c. Buttons are similar to links, and may be used to connect individual products from Shoppingbee.com to your website.

- Search for any product across any category
- Select choice of button from the options listed
- Highlight the code in the text box, and copy
- Paste the code in your website

d. You may also use the Search Bar to browse through Shoppingbee.com products right from your website.

- Highlight and copy the code given in the text box
- Paste the code in your website to get the Shoppingbee.com search tool
Why can't I see my earnings for recent orders?
Your earnings for a product will reflect in your account only when the order is delivered. Usually, you should be able to see your earnings for a product approx. 3-5 days after the order is placed
What are the payment methods?
Affiliates have an option of receiving payment via
a. Gift Voucher
b. EFT
If affiliates select payment via gift voucher, Shoppingbee will accrue and withhold referral fees until the total amount due is at least Rs 250.00. If affiliates select payment via EFT, Shoppingbee will accrue and withhold referral fees until the total amount due is at least Rs 1000.00. Gift vouchers may be redeemed against purchase from www.Shoppingbee.com
