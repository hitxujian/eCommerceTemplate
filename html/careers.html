Why Shoppingbee

Shoppingbee was started by a group of IIT/IIM graduates in early 2007 and is headquartered in Bangalore with regional offices in New Delhi, Mumbai and Chennai. The company is funded by top tier Venture Capital Funds.

In addition to accelerated growth opportunities, we foster openness, trust and respect in the workplace. We're a warm and fun-filled place to work; we're passionate about sport and music, debate and design and about getting things right. And while we are small, we are growing rapidly and our growth philosophy is to hire and retain the best. To this end, we offer all employees a culture of fun and learning, and a very competitive package of pay and benefits. Some highlights of our offer are as follows:

  • 11 paid holidays in a year for employees to celebrate national events and festivals with their families, along with 18 days of paid annual leave to help maintain work-life balance & 12 days of sick leave. Additional leave types that include paid paternity leave of up to 10 business days per annum.
  • Every employee is assigned two days of professional development opportunities per year in the form of trainings, certifications, seminar and conference participation opportunities etc.
  • Shoppingbee provides medical insurance to all its employees. 100% of the premium for this is paid by Shoppingbee. This provides reimbursement per annum of up to Rs. 2 Lakhs per family including parents/in-laws.
  • All employees are eligible for Group Personal Accident insurance. Due to bodily injury or death as a result of an accident, GPA insurance provides benefit of upto Rs. 10 Lakhs or twice the CTC whichever is higher per defined payment schedule. Shoppingbee pays 100% of the premium.
  • We foster a culture of fun and transparency with events such as our Monthly All Hands Meets, Sports @ Shoppingbee initiative, Founding Day Annual Celebration for employees and their families and lots more.
  • We encourage the generation of smart ideas by our employees from across teams and levels - the best idea of the month receives an I pad!

CONTENT WRITER

Who we are:

Do you want to be a part of a lively team of creative writers who love fashion? We are working to improve the fashion content of our website and need writers who have a great knowledge of brands and fashion products, as well as excellent language skills. Shoppingbee’s aim is to educate people about fashion and about how to look good!

Business unit and role overview:

The catalogue department provides the written and visual content for all products uploaded on Shoppingbee.com. Content Writers are required to provide original descriptions for every product and give style advice.

Responsibilities:
  • Write/proof read/edit Shoppingbee.com website product descriptions – identify the attributes of the product and write with a clear understanding of the target audience with the right tone of voice
  • Develop highly effective, original and targeted content for the promotion and sale of Shoppingbee products online
  • Work with various internal and external teams to get hold of the correct product information
Skills / experience:
  • 2-4 years of content writing experience online or offline. Fashion background preferred. Expert writing skills, editing and proof reading abilities
  • Ability to write terse, attention grabbing and professional copy that puts the message across
  • Creative bent of mind with strong visualization and idea generation skills
  • Ability to deliver within tight deadlines
  • Excellent relationship management skills
  • Aggressive and willing to work in a startup
Qualifications:

BA/MA English Literature, journalism or related or BA/MA Fashion Design, Fashion Communication or related

Your career:

There is plenty of room to develop your writing skills in this role, and space to progress into an editor position. Regular writing workshops and training on new categories and products ensure writers are constantly upgrading their abilities.

SENIOR SOFTWARE ENGINEER

Shoppingbee.com is ranked among the leading platforms to foster e-commerce companies in. The company was started by a group of IIT/IIM graduates in 2007 and is headquartered in Bangalore.

Business Unit and Role Overview:

Once orders are placed on the portal Shoppingbee.com, starts a very elaborate order fulfillment and delivery workflow. A myriad of well integrated subsystems that handle these workflows, constitute the Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) and Supply Chain Management (SCM) software. These systems run 24x7 to manage warehouse and logistics operations, deal with thousands of orders in a day, record-keep tens of millions of items in inventory and are able to handle hundreds of millions of transactions in the system. A high-caliber Engineering team has built ERP systems from ground up on highly scalable Service Oriented Architecture (SOA). This group leverages cutting edge technologies to continuously optimize the inventory life while driving down storage and delivery costs and improving customer experience.

Responsibilities:
  • Write maintainable/scalable/efficient code.
  • Design and architect technical solutions for the business problems of a very large e-commerce portal.
  • Work in cross-functional team, collaborating with peers during entire SDLC.
  • Follow coding standards, unit-testing, code reviews etc.
  • Follow release cycles and commitment to deadlines.
Requirements:
  • Experience level of 4 to 7 years of experience in very large scale applications
  • Strong problem solving skills, data structures and algorithms.
  • Experience with distributed systems handling large amount of data.
  • Excellent coding skills in Java/J2EE.
  • Very good understanding of Web Technologies
  • Very good understanding of any RDBMS and/or messaging
Qualifications:

B Tech/BE or M.Tech/ME in Computer Science or equivalent from a reputed college.

Your Career:

Being a relatively small sized Engineering team, individuals carry a lot of responsibility on their shoulders. Coming up with the designs of features, evaluating external libraries and frameworks, enhancing existing platform infrastructure, reviewing major architectural decisions, planning and executing deployments with minimal system downtimes is part of everyday job. For an individual with hunger to solve problems and who thrives on challenges, the opportunities at this place are practically limitless.
